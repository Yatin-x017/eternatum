## 2025-05-15 - [Accessibility Consistency Pattern]
**Learning:** Found that while some components (like UserScoreboard) implemented proper ARIA attributes for progress bars, others (like LearnByBuilding) did not. Consistency in accessibility patterns is crucial for a reliable assistive technology experience.
**Action:** Always cross-reference similar components (e.g., any component with a 'progress' visual) to ensure accessibility attributes are applied consistently across the design system.
